ABOUT

Our advanced circuit board prototyping facility utilizes the latest technology in circuit board printers to prototype short runs of extremely high end boards. Zahn Innovation Center prototyping service gives you complete control over your order. Our online PCB prototype shop can turn even the most complicated designs into working prototypes quickly and with minimal fuss.

Making a prototype PCB is your chance to see the first functional version of your design. Before going to volume or batch production, this is your chance to iron out any design kinks, improve the allocation of materials and impress stakeholders or investors with an interest in your project.

Letting an expert handle the production of your printed circuit board prototype doesn’t mean giving up control. Our free consultation and circuit printing facility gives you an unmatched level of influence over your order and your finished product.
